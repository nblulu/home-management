House Rules & Standards

The expectations that keep our home running peacefully — posted where everyone can see.

🧹 Cleanliness Standards

1.If you make a mess, you clean it up — before leaving the area.
2.Dishes go in the sink or dishwasher immediately after eating — never left on the table.
3.Shoes OFF at the front door, always.
4.Clothes go in the hamper — not the floor.
5.Beds made every morning before breakfast.
6.Toys and belongings must be in bedrooms or their homes by bedtime.

🍽️ Kitchen Rules

1.Ask before eating anything outside scheduled meal/snack times.
2.Wipe the counter after you use it — even for just a snack.
3.Karam may use the microwave and toaster independently. Stovetop needs an adult present.
4.Put ingredients back where they belong after using them.
5.No drinks in the living room except water in a closed bottle.

📱 Screen Time Rules

1.No screens at the dinner table — ever. Family time is screen-free.
2.Karam: Homework & chores FIRST, screens after. School nights: 7–8pm only.
3.Naya: max 1 hr per day, age-appropriate content, in the living room.
4.All devices charge in the kitchen overnight — not in bedrooms.
5.Weekend bonus screen time is earned with completed star charts.

🕐 Routines & Timing

1.Everyone follows their posted schedule — check the chart, not Mom.
2.Backpacks are packed the NIGHT before — never the morning of.
3.Bedtimes are not negotiable on school nights.
4.The Sunday Family Meeting is non-optional — 15 minutes, everyone attends.
5.If you're running late on a chore, communicate immediately — don't just skip it.

💛 Kindness & Respect

1.Speak kindly to every member of the family — no yelling, name-calling, or put-downs.
2.Karam helps Naya — he is her example. Patience with her always.
3.Say thank you when someone helps you or cooks for you.
4.Knock before entering closed doors.
5.When Mom or Dad says "family time," all activities stop.

⭐ Consequences & Rewards

✅Completed chores = stars / stickers added at evening check-in.
✅Full week of chores = bonus privilege (movie night, extra screen time, special outing).
❌Skipped chore = no screen time that day, chore still must be completed.
❌Disrespectful behavior = loss of privilege for that day, conversation with both parents.
💬Rules can be discussed at Sunday Family Meeting — respectfully and calmly.
